# Business Requirements

## Problem

Retail companies lose money because of:

- Overstocking
- Understocking

## Objective

Predict future demand for products and recommend inventory levels.

## Inputs

- Historical sales
- Product information
- Calendar information
- Promotions
- Pricing

## Outputs

- Demand forecast
- Inventory recommendation

## Success Metrics

- MAE
- RMSE
- MAPE

## Business Benefits

- Reduce stockouts
- Reduce inventory costs
- Improve customer satisfaction
